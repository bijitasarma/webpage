Research Areas

Quantum Control

We utilize deep reinforcement learning to discover optimal control pulses for open quantum systems. This includes stabilizing qubits against noise and preparing non-classical states in non-linear potentials.

Error Correction

Our work focuses on measurement-based feedback strategies for Quantum Error Correction (QEC). We are exploring autonomous QEC schemes that can be implemented on near-term hardware.

Optomechanics

Investigating ground-state cooling and photon antibunching in cavity optomechanical systems. Tailoring light-matter interaction for quantum sensing.

Hardware Efficient Gates

Designing fast and high-fidelity quantum gates for superconducting qubits using machine learning ansatz, targeting architectures for the Munich Quantum Valley.
